Welcome To Eve Schultz Fine Arts

Eve Schultz grew up in the thumb of Michigan on her family's farm and about an hour from Michigan's Lake Huron's shores. Days outside whether by the lake or in the arena with her horses inspired her to start sketching.

With Eve's father introducing the way into the world of art for her, she is otherwise 100% self taught. She has won several awards for her art at the Tuscola County Fair and hopes to enter more art shows in the future.

Eve's love for the traditional ranching and cowboying is strong. Waking up before the crack of dawn, bringing in yearling calves from the fields, breaking in colts before dusk. Eve hopes to show this authentic life style as it is - real and alive.
